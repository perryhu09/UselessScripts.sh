READ ME 

Welcome to the TCD, Troy’s Culinary Department. Unfortunately, the company has had a security breach in these past recent days. The specific computer they have hired you to secure is a computer used for server administration. The computer has an installed LAMP stack and runs Ubuntu 16. Unfortunately, the security breach has left this LAMP stack vulnerable and deprived it of its functionality.

Unfortunately, documentation for this computer is nonexistent. Therefore, the company cannot give specific guidelines on what to secure in the system. They have hired you for your prior knowledge of computer environments. Secure this system to the best of your ability. The company demand is that, after you have finished your task, the LAMP stack and the computer itself will be best prepared against any future cyber attacks. 

The previous server administrator has also left a variety of media files on the system after being fired for putting memes on the website. Although not necessarily a security hazard, the TCD has requested you to remove these and any other things not relating to the functionality of the computer or the LAMP stack.

If you succeed, you will be considered for admission into one of Troy’s top culinary teams. Good luck!
